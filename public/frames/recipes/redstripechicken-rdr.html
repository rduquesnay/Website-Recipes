Step:
  1. Cut the chicken into serving pieces.
  2. De-deseed green bell pepper and cut into slices.
  3. chop up onion.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in dutch pot.
  5. Brown the chicken in oil (both sides).
  6. Remove the chicken.
  7. Pour off some of the oil.
  8. Leave 2 table spoon of oil in the pot.
  9. Return the chicken to the pot.
  10. Add the coconut milk and red strip beer.
  11. Once the pot is boiling reduce to heat to a simmer.
  12. Cook for 30 minutes.
  13. Add onion, bell pepper.
  14. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  15. Let simmer for 15-20 minutes as the liquid reduces and thickens.
  16. If the liquid evaporates before the chicken is done replenish more Red Stripe beer.
  17. Serve while hot.
